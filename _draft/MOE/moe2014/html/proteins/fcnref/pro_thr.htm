Syntax

    Z_score = Hydrophobic_Fitness_Z [ res_names, mol_data, align_pos ]

Description

Calculates the degree to which the hydrophobic residues of a sequence form a distinct compact core when threaded onto a given 3D conformation.

res_names
The sequence is supplied as a vector of 3-letter residue name tokens.

mol_data
Protein in MOE molecule format (as described in mol_Extract) onto which the sequence is threaded.

align_pos
Optional 2 element vector of alignment positions: 
    [align_pos_res, align_pos_mol]
  • align_pos_res is the alignment positions assigned to the residues in the sequence res_names.
  • align_pos_mol is the alignment positions assigned to the residues in the protein mol_data.

The value returned by Hydrophobic_Fitness_Z is the difference, in units of standard deviation, between the hydrophobic fitness score of the original query sequence and the mean of a distribution of fitness scores constructed by repeated random permutations of the query sequence and recalculations of the hydrophobic fitness score. The hydrophobic fitness score is an evaluation of a sequence-to-fold compatibility function.
