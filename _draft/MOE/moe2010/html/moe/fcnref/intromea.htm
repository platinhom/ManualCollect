Meter and Measurement Functions

Various steric measurements such as measuring distances, angles or dihedral angles can be made on molecules. For instance, you can measure bond lengths or the backbone dihedral angles that determine protein structure. Graphical meters can be displayed, showing the value of the measured quantity. Meters are dynamically updated to reflect current values.

The following functions pertain to the measurement of meters and dihedral angles:

aAngle
aAngleDeg
aDihedralCW
aDihedralCCW
aDist
aSetDihedralCW
aSetDihedralCCW 		Meters
MeterChanged
MeterCreate
