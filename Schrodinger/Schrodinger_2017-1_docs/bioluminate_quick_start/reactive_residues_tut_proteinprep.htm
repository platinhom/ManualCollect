Preparing the Protein

1. Click Protein Preparation Wizard on the Favorites toolbar.

The Protein Preparation Wizard panel opens.

2. Enter 2pcy in the PDB text box, and click Import.

The 2PCY structure is imported into the project and displayed in the Workspace. This panel provides a wide range of options for preparing proteins for modeling: fixing structural defects and making assignments required by modeling applications. For this exercise, the default settings will be used.

3. Click Preprocess.

The structure is processed, and a new structure is added to the project and displayed in the Workspace, replacing the imported structure.

You can close the Protein Preparation Wizard panel.
