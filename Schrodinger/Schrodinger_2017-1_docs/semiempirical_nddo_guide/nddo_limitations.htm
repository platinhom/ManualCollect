Limitations on Semiempirical NDDO Calculations

The following limitations should be noted:

  • Spin states higher than sextet are not available.

  • ESP and SUPER calculations cannot be run with the MNDO/d method.

  • UHF (unsupported keyword) cannot be used for ESP, MULLIKEN, or SUPER calculations.
