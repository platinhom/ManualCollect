The QSite Potential Tab

The Potential tab provides options for the definition of the potential energy functions used in the molecular mechanics part of the calculation. One of these (continuum solvation) affects the QM potential energy as well. For detailed information on the options in this tab, see QSite Panel — Potential Tab.
