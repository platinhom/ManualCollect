Visualization Tools in the Materials Science Suite

The graphical interface has a number of visualization tools for display of the results of calculations.

  • Plotting spectra—plot UV/Vis, infrared, or VCD spectra from Jaguar, or imported as raw data (Plotting Spectra, Spectrum Plot Panel)

  • Plotting potential energy surfaces—plot the results of a coordinate scan in 1 or 2 dimensions (Plotting Coordinate Scan Results)

  • Scatter plots of properties—plot two properties, with representation of two more in terms of color and size of plot symbols (Scatter Plot Panel)

  • Property surfaces—display surfaces generated by Jaguar, such as charge or spin density, molecular orbitals, electrostatic potential (Manage Surfaces Panel)

Plotting capabilities are also included for analysis of the output generated with some of the Materials Science panels.

  • Stress-strain plot (Stress Strain Viewer Panel)
  • Density as a function of temperature for Tg and coefficient of thermal expansion (Thermophysical Properties Analysis Panel)
  • Optoelectronics properties (Optoelectronics Results Panel)
  • Optoelectronics genetic optimization results (Optoelectronics Genetic Optimization Viewer Panel)
  • Periodic DFT: density of states, band structure (Density of States Viewer Panel, Band Structure Viewer Panel)
  • Crosslinked polymer properties (Crosslink Polymers Viewer Panel)
  • Self-organizing map for properties (Materials Explorer Panel)
