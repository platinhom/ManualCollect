Structure Optimization in the Materials Science Suite

Tools for minimization of the energy of structures with respect to their geometry (geometry optimization) are available under Tasks → General Modeling → Minimization. These include

  • force-field minimization of single structures with MacroModel (MacroModel Minimizations)

  • force-field minimization of periodic structures with Desmond (Desmond Minimizations)

  • semiempirical quantum-mechanical minimizations of single structures (Semiempirical NDDO Guide).

  • ab initio quantum-mechanical minimizations of single structures (Jaguar Optimizations and Scans).

  • periodic DFT minimization of atom positions in periodic structures (Quantum ESPRESSO Calculations Panel)

Conformational search facilities are available under Tasks → General Modeling → Conformational Search, and include a general search facility (MacroModel Conformational Searches), and one specifically designed for locating conformations of macrocycles.
