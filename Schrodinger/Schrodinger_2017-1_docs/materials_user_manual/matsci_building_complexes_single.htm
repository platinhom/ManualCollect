Building a Single Organometallic Complex

You can build a single organometallic complex with a single metal center, in octahedral, tetrahedral, or square planar geometry, in the Build Single Complex panel, which you open by choosing Tasks → Materials → Build → Single Complex in the Task Tool.

There are three main steps to building an organometallic complex:

1. Choose the metal and the coordination geometry.
2. Choose the ligands.
3. Choose the arrangement of ligands in the complex.
