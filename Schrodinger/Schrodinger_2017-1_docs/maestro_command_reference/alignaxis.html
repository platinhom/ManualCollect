alignaxis

Specifies a pair of atoms of a molecule to align the molecule to one of X, Y, or Z axis.

Syntax: alignaxis axis= y | z [<atom1> <atom2> ]

Options:

axis

This option determines to which axis will the molecule be aligned: x=1, y=2, z=3.

Valid values: x
y
z
Default value: x

Operands:

<atom1> <atom2>

The first atom will be put at the origine and the second atom will be at whatever the distance is on the chosen axis.
