monitordistance

Specifies a pair of atoms to have their distance monitored during a MacroModel dynamics simulation.

Syntax: monitordistance [<atom1> <atom2> ]

Operands:

<atom1> <atom2>

The atom numbers of two atoms which are to have the distance between them monitored during a dynamics simulation. Note that specifying a-b is the same as b-a
