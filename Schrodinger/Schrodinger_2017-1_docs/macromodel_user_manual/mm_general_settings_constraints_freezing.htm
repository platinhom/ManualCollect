Freezing Atoms in MacroModel

“Frozen” atoms have no forces acting on them and do not move from their initial position in the structure. Frozen atoms do influence other, non-frozen atoms in the structure, mostly through nonbonded interactions. Frozen atoms differ from constrained atoms in that constrained atoms may move slightly.

To freeze atoms, select them in the Atoms tab, then select the check boxes in the Frozen column of the table. To freeze multiple atoms simultaneously, select the rows in the table, and then control-click the check box in the Frozen column for one of the rows. To unfreeze an atom, click its check box again (or use control-click for multiple atoms).
