Free-Energy Perturbation in MacroModel

  • See the FEGA, FEIA, FEAV, FESA, and FESU commands; also the didactic write-up in Free Energy Simulations with MacroModel. Free-energy perturbation may be carried out using mixed-mode Monte Carlo / Stochastic Dynamics, as well as SD or MD. When used in network-distributed mode, each process runs a separate window.
