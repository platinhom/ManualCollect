Several opcodes have been discussed in previous chapters. Only the opcodes relevant to the MC/SD procedure are described below.

MINI: Minimize the structure before the dynamics simulation.

MCNV: The number of torsion angles to vary during the MC portion of the simulation. Here, between one and three torsion variations are specified.

TORS: Specify the torsion angles to vary by atom number.

MCSD: Specify MC/SD sampling at 300 K with an MC to SD step ratio of 1:1.

MDIT: Specify dynamics simulation at a temperature of 300 K.

MDYN: Perform equilibration dynamics run for 1 ps.

MDSA: Sample 20 structures during the following full dynamics run.

MDYN: Perform full MC/SD dynamics run for 10 ps at 1.5 fs intervals.
