Zooming and Fitting

To zoom in or out on the Workspace structures, do one of the following:

  • Rotate the mouse wheel.
  • On a trackpad, use the pinch gesture.
  • Hold down the middle and right (2nd and 3rd) mouse buttons and move the mouse to the right (zoom in) or left (zoom out).
  • Hold down the SHIFT and CTRL keys and the middle mouse button and move the mouse to the right (zoom in) or left (zoom out).
  • Press the K key to zoom in or J key to zoom out.
  • Choose View → Zoom In or View → Zoom Out.

Fitting to the Workspace is another form of zooming in or out, so that a group of atoms fills the Workspace. This can be done from the toolbar, in the Fit section, and also from the View menu. The View → Fit submenu has three items: All, Selected, and Ligand, which fit these atoms to the Workspace. Choosing All is useful if you want to fit the full atom set to the Workspace, but don't want to clear your atom selection. By default, there are keyboard shortcuts for Selected (Z) and Ligand (L). The other two items are for auto-fitting the Workspace when the content changes, to all atoms or to the ligand.

When a project is closed, all view information (rotation, translation, and zoom) is saved with the project and restored when it is opened again.

The Preferences Panel has a number of preferences that affect zooming and fitting to the Workspace, under Workspace – Fitting. You can customize the mouse actions for zoom in the Customize Mouse Actions Panel, which you open with Maestro → Customize → Mouse Actions.
