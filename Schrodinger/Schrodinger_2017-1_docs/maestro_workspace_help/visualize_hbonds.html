Visualize H-Bonds Panel

Display hydrogen bonds to the selected atoms, and label them with distances and donor-acceptor angle.

To open this panel, choose Tasks → Structure Analysis → Visualize H-Bonds in the Task Tool browser.

Visualize H-Bonds Panel Features

  • Distance text box
  • Display buttons
  • Reset Panel button
Distance text box

Set the distance from the selected atoms for detection of H-bonds. Any atoms that fall within this distance are checked for hydrogen bonds, not just the selected atoms. (Note: this is not the threshold for defining an H-bond.)

Display buttons

These buttons turn on or off the display of H-bonds with their distances or angles in the Workspace.

Show or hide hydrogen bonds and the H-bond distance, from the hydrogen to the acceptor. The bonds are marked in yellow.
Show or hide the donor-acceptor distance. The distance is marked in purple.
Show or hide the donor-acceptor angles. The line between the D-H bond centroid and the A-X bond centroid is shown in green, with the angles (D-H-A, H-A-X) shown at either end.
Reset Panel button

Reset the panel to its default settings.
