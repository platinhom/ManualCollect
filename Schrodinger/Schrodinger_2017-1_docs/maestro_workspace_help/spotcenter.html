Spot Centering

Spot-centering changes the view so that the selected atom is at the center of the Workspace, and makes the chosen atom the center of rotation for the camera angle. Spot-centering does not affect the molecular coordinates.

To spot-center an atom, middle-click on the atom. Middle-click on a ribbon segment to spot-center on the alpha carbon for the corresponding residue.
