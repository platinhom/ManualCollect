Hybrid Functionals

The functionals listed below all contain a component of Hartree-Fock exchange, but do not include partitioning of the Coulomb interaction operator (range separation).

  • B3LYP1Keyword dftname = b3lyp in the gen section.: Exchange: exact HF, Slater local functional [34], Becke 1988 nonlocal gradient correction [37]; correlation: Vosko-Wilk-Nusair (VWN) local functional [35], Lee-Yang-Parr local and nonlocal functional [38]. See refs. [32] and [33]. D3 correction available.

  • B3PW912Keyword dftname = b3pw91 in the gen section.: Exchange: exact HF, Slater local functional [34], Becke 1988 nonlocal gradient correction [37]; correlation: Perdew-Wang 1991 local and GGA-II nonlocal functional [36]; D3 correction available.

  • B3P863Keyword dftname = b3p86 in the gen section.: Exchange: exact HF, Slater local exchange functional [34], Becke 1988 nonlocal gradient correction [37]; correlation: Vosko-Wilk-Nusair (VWN) local functional [35], Perdew 1986 nonlocal gradient correction [40]

  • B1B954Keyword dftname = b1b95 in the gen section.: Becke's hybrid functional from 1996 containing 28% HF exact exchange [41]. D3 correction available.

  • BB1K5Keyword dftname = bb1k in the gen section.: Reparametrization of Becke's 1996 hybrid functional for reaction barriers, containing 42% HF exact exchange [56]

  • B97-16Keyword dftname = b97-1 in the gen section.: Reparametrization of Becke’s 1997 hybrid functional [42] by Hamprecht, Cohen, Tozer, and Handy [45]

  • B987Keyword dftname = b98 in the gen section.: Becke’s 1998 hybrid functional including the Laplacian of the density and kinetic energy density terms as well as gradient terms [43]

  • SB988Keyword dftname = sb98 in the gen section.: Schmider and Becke reparametrization of Becke’s 1998 functional [44]

  • BHandH9Keyword dftname = bhandh in the gen section.: 50% exact HF exchange, 50% Slater local exchange functional [34]

  • BHandHLYP10Keyword dftname = bhandhlyp in the gen section.: Exchange: 50% exact HF exchange, 50% Slater local exchange functional [34]; correlation: Lee-Yang-Parr local and nonlocal functionals [38]

  • X3LYP11Keyword dftname = x3lyp in the gen section.: Extension of B3LYP by Xu and Goddard to include Perdew-Wang 1991 gradient correction exchange functional [36], with exchange parametrized to fit Gaussian exchange density [55]

  • MPW1PW9112Keyword dftname = mpw1pw91 in the gen section.: Hybrid functional including modification of Perdew-Wang gradient correction exchange functional, by Adamo and Barone [52]. Exchange: Exact HF exchange, Slater local functional [34], Perdew-Wang 1991 gradient correction functional [36]; correlation: Perdew-Wang 1991 GGA-II local and nonlocal functionals [36]

  • MPW1K13Keyword dftname = mpw1k in the gen section.: Reoptimization of mPW1PW91 functional parameter for prediction of reaction barrier heights, by Lynch, Fast, Harris, and Truhlar [54]

  • PWB6K14Keyword dftname = pwb6k in the gen section.: Reoptimization of MPWB1K functionals for simultaneous accuracy of bond energies, barrier heights, and nonbonded interactions, by Zhao and Truhlar [56].

  • PW6B9515Keyword dftname = pw6b95 in the gen section.: Reoptimization of MPW1B95 functionals for simultaneous accuracy of bond energies, barrier heights, and nonbonded interactions, by Zhao and Truhlar [56]. D3 correction available.

  • PBE016Keyword dftname = pbe0 in the gen section.: Functional due to Adamo and Barone [53] based on PBE functional. 25% exact HF exchange, 75% PBE non-local exchange. Correlation: Perdew-Burke-Ernzerhof [47] local and nonlocal correlation. Also known as PBE1PBE. D3 correction available.

  • M0517Keyword dftname = m05 in the gen section.: Hybrid functional parametrized for broad accuracy, including noncovalent interactions, kinetics, and interactions with metals, by Zhao, Schultz, and Truhlar [[58], [59]]. D3 correction available.

  • M05-2X18Keyword dftname = m05-2x in the gen section.: Hybrid functional with larger HF exchange component, similar to M05 but parametrized for nonmetals, by Zhao, Schultz, and Truhlar [[58], [59]]. D3 correction available.

  • M06-HF19Keyword dftname = m06-hf in the gen section.: Zhao and Truhlar functional with full HF exchange and M06 local functionals that eliminates long-range self-interaction. [61]. D3 correction available.

  • M0620Keyword dftname = m06 in the gen section.: Zhao and Truhlar functional, parametrized with metallic systems, for organometallic and inorganic chemistry and noncovalent interactions [62].D3 correction available.

  • M06-2X21Keyword dftname = m06-2x in the gen section.: Zhao and Truhlar functional, parametrized for nonmetals, for main-group thermochemistry, kinetics, noncovalent interactions, and electronic excitation energies to valence and Rydberg states [62]. D3 correction available.

  • O3LYP22Keyword dftname = o3lyp in the gen section.: Exchange: exact HF, Slater local functional [34], OPTX nonlocal functional of Handy and Cohen [48]; correlation: Lee-Yang-Parr local and nonlocal functionals [38]

  • M08-HX23Keyword dftname = m08-hx in the gen section.: Zhao and Truhlar functional, constraining the reduced density gradient to exact exchange and correlation functional forms through second order. Parametrized on a broad range of properties [63].

  • M08-SO24Keyword dftname = m08-hx in the gen section.: Zhao and Truhlar functional, constraining the reduced density gradient to exact exchange and correlation functional forms through second order. Parametrized on a broad range of properties [63].

  • MN1525Keyword dftname = mn15 in the gen section.: Nonseparable gradient approximation from Truhlar and coworkers, with emphasis on multireference systems, barrier heights, noncovalent interactions and excitation energies. [66].
