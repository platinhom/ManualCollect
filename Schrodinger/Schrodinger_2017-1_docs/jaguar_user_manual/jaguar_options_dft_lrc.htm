Long-Range-Corrected Functionals

These functionals include range separation using a partitioning of the Coulomb operator (see Long-Range-Corrected Density Functionals) to partition the HF exchange into short-range and long-range parts. The units of the range separation parameter w are bohr-1. Short-range and long-range exchange functionals are obtained from the full functional either by the method of Iikura et al. [68] or that of Henderson et al. [70]. The modified versions are prefixed with SR- or LR-. The w value can be changed by setting the lrc-omega keyword in the input file.

  • LRC-BLYP1Keyword dftname = lrc-blyp in the gen section.: Yanai, Tew, and Handy functional [69]. Short-range exchange: SR-Becke 88 [37]; long-range exchange: LR-HF; w=0.33; correlation: LYP [38].

  • CAM-B3LYP2Keyword dftname = cam-b3lyp in the gen section.: Yanai, Tew, and Handy functional [69]. Short-range exchange: 19% SR-HF, 81% SR-Becke 88 [37]; long-range exchange: 65% LR-HF, 35% LR-Becke 88 [37]; w=0.33; correlation: LYP [38]. D3 correction available.

  • uPBE3Keyword dftname = upbe in the gen section. No reference; values from NWChem.: Long-range corrected PBE functional. Short range exchange: SR-PBE; long range exchange: LR-HF; w=0.30; correlation: PBE [47].

  • uPBE04Keyword dftname = upbe0 in the gen section. No reference; values from NWChem.: Long-range corrected PBE hybrid. Short range exchange: 25% SR-HF, 75% SR-PBE; long range exchange: LR-HF; w=0.30; correlation: PBE [47].

  • wPBE5Keyword dftname = wpbe in the gen section.: Vydrov and Scuseria functional [71]. D3 correction available.

  • HSE038Keyword dftname = hse03 in the gen section.: Heyd, Scuseria, and Ernzerhof functional [73].   • M1111Keyword dftname = m11 in the gen section.: Peverati and Truhlar functional [76]. Short range exchange: 42.8% SR-M11 [76] and HF; long-range exchange: HF; w=0.25; correlation: M11 [76].
