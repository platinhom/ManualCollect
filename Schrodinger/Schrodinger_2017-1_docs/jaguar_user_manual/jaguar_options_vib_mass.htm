Setting Atomic Masses for Jaguar Frequencies

For frequency calculations the atomic mass used for each element is that of its most abundant isotope by default.1Keyword massav = 0 in the gen section. However, you can choose to use an average of the isotopic masses, weighted by the abundance of the isotopes, by selecting Average isotopic masses2Keyword massav = 1 in the gen section. from the Atomic masses option menu.
