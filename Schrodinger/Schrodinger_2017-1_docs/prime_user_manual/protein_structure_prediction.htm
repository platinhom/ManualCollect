Protein Structure Prediction

The Prime Structure Prediction (PSP) workflow offers the most flexibility of the interfaces available for homology modeling in Maestro. You can make use of multiple templates, generate and edit the alignment, and build single-chain or multimer models. The workflow is presented as a series of steps in a wizard-like interface. These steps are described in the topics listed below.
