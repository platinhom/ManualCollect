Deleting Remaining Unwanted Waters

After deleting water molecules beyond a certain distance from the ligand, examine the Workspace and delete any remaining water molecules you do not want to keep:

  1. Choose Molecules from the Set selection scope button on the toolbar:

  2. Click on a water oxygen and press the Del key to delete that water molecule.
