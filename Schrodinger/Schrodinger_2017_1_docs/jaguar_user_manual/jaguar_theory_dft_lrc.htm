Long-Range-Corrected Density Functionals

Most common density functionals cannot correctly describe the distance dependence of charge-transfer excitation energies since the non-local Hartree-Fock exchange is not 100% included. One way to solve this problem is to preserve the form of common GGAs and hybrid functionals at short range while applying 100% Hartree-Fock exchange at long range.
