Molecular Properties from SM6 and SM8 Solvated Calculations

When an SM6 calculation is carried out in Jaguar, several additional molecular properties are automatically calculated because they are necessary for the SM6 calculation.

  • Löwdin Population Analysis— Löwdin populations [227, 228] are computed for each atom in the gas phase and in the solution phase.

  • Redistributed Löwdin Population Analysis—When basis sets containing diffuse functions (that is, 6-31+G* or 6-31+G**) are specified with SM6, redistributed Löwdin populations [229] are computed for each atom in the gas phase and in the solution phase.

  • Mayer Bond Orders—Mayer bond orders [230–232] are computed between each pair of atoms in the gas phase and in the solution phase. To print the Mayer bond order matrix, you must specify ip378=2 in the gen section.
