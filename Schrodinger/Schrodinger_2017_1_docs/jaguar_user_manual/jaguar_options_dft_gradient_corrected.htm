Gradient-Corrected Functionals

The functions listed below all contain terms that are functionals of the gradient of the density as well as the density itself. Some functionals also include second derivatives of the density or the kinetic energy density. These functionals do not contain Hartree-Fock exchange or partitioning of the Coulomb interaction operator (range separation).

  • BLYP1Keyword dftname = blyp in the gen section.: Exchange: Slater local functional [34], Becke 1988 nonlocal gradient correction [37]; correlation: Lee-Yang-Parr local and nonlocal functionals [38]; D3 correction available

  • BPW912Keyword dftname = bpw91 in the gen section.: Exchange: Slater local functional [34], Becke 1988 nonlocal gradient correction [37]; correlation: Perdew-Wang 1991 GGA-II local and nonlocal functionals [36]

  • BP863Keyword dftname = bp86 in the gen section.: Exchange: Slater local functional [34], Becke 1988 non-local gradient correction [37]; correlation: Perdew-Zunger 1981 local functional [39], Perdew 1986 gradient correction functional [40]; D3 correction available.

  • BP86-VWN54Keyword dftname = bp86-vwn5 in the gen section.: Exchange: Slater local functional [34], Becke 1988 nonlocal gradient correction [37]; correlation: Vosko-Wilk-Nusair (VWN) local functional [35], Perdew 1986 gradient correction functional [40]

  • PWPW915Keyword dftname = pwpw91 in the gen section.: Exchange: Slater local functional [34], Perdew-Wang 1991 gradient correction functional [36]; correlation: Perdew-Wang 1991 GGA-II local and nonlocal functionals [36]

  • PBE6Keyword dftname = pbe in the gen section.: Perdew-Burke-Ernzerhof local and nonlocal exchange and correlation functional [47]; D3 correction available.

  • HCTH4077Keyword dftname = hcth407 in the gen section.: Hamprecht-Cohen-Tozer-Handy functional [45] including local and nonlocal exchange and correlation, reparametrized with a training set of 407 molecules by Boese and Handy [46]

  • M06-L8Keyword dftname = m06-l in the gen section.: Parameterization by Zhao and Truhlar of various functionals that include gradient and kinetic energy spin density functionals [60]

  • N129Keyword dftname = n12 in the gen section.: Parameterization by Peverati and Truhlar that combines gradient and density terms in a nonseparable manner, parametrized to give good accuracy for solid-state and molecular energies and structures [64]

  • MN12-L10Keyword dftname = mn12-l in the gen section.: Parameterization by Peverati and Truhlar that includes gradient and kinetic energy spin density functionals, with broad applicability [65]

  • GAM11Keyword dftname = gam in the gen section.: Parameterization by Truhlar and coworkers similar to N12, parametrized with smoothness constraints to give better accuracy for molecules, particularly transition metal complexes [64]

  • MN15-L12Keyword dftname = mn15-l in the gen section.: Rearameterization of the MN12-L functional against a broader database and with constraints to ensure smooth functions of geometry [67]

  • OLYP13Keyword dftname = olyp in the gen section.: Exchange: Slater local functional [34], OPTX nonlocal exchange of Handy and Cohen [48]; correlation: Lee-Yang-Parr local and nonlocal functionals [38]; D3 correction available.

  • B97-D14Keyword dftname = b97-d in the gen section.: Modified Becke 1997 functional [42] with Grimme’s long-range dispersion correction, useful for noncovalent interactions [49]. D3 correction also available.
