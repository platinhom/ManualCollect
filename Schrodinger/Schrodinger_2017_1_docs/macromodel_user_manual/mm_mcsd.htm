Monte Carlo/Stochastic Dynamics with MacroModel

Monte Carlo/Stochastic Dynamics (MC/SD) performs constant temperature calculations that take advantage of the strengths of Monte Carlo methods for quickly introducing large changes in a few degrees of freedom, and stochastic dynamics for its effective local sampling of collective motions. MC/SD is a good general choice for studying a system at constant temperature.
