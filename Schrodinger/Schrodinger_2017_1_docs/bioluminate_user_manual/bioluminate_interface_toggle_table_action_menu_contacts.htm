Displaying Polar Contacts with the Toggle Table

You can turn on or off the display of polar contacts (hydrogen bonds) between various groups of atoms from the Polar Contacts submenu. The display is turned on with A → Polar Contacts → Find; it is turned off with A → Polar Contacts → Remove All. The atom groupings are:

  • Within Object
  • Involving Side Chains
  • Involving Solvent
  • Excluding Solvent
  • Excluding Main Chain
  • Excluding Intra-Main Chain
  • Just Intra-Side Chain
  • Just Intra-Main Chain
  • To Other Atoms In Entry
  • To Other Atoms In Entry Excluding Solvent
  • To Any Atoms
  • To Any Atoms Excluding Solvent

Each menu choice clears any previous choice before applying the new choice.

If you want more flexibility in choosing the atom groups between which polar contacts are shown, you can use the Non-Bonded Interactions panel. Choose Style → H-Bonds and Halogen Bonds from the menu bar at the top of the Workspace to open this panel. You can also use the HBonds toolbar button on the Measurements toolbar.
