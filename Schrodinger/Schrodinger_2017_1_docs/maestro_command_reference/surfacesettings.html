surfacesettings

This keyword is used to set various options associated with surface types.

Syntax: surfacesettings default_bluecolor= <n> default_color= gray | dark_blue | blue | light_blue | aquamarine | turquoise | spring_green | dark_green | green | lime_green | yellow_green | yellow | orange | maroon | red | pink | plum | magenta | blue_violet | white default_greencolor= <n> default_paired_bluecolor= <n> default_paired_color= gray | dark_blue | blue | light_blue | aquamarine | turquoise | spring_green | dark_green | green | lime_green | yellow_green | yellow | orange | maroon | red | pink | plum | magenta | blue_violet | white default_paired_greencolor= <n> default_paired_redcolor= <n> default_redcolor= <n> [<model_name> ]

Options:

Valid values: integers
Default value: 0
