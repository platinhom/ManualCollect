Saving and Restoring the View

When you rotate, translate, or zoom in on the structures in the Workspace, the actual coordinates of the structures are not changed, only the view of the structures is changed: the "camera angle". Maestro stores the current view as a transformation that is applied to the coordinates. You can save a particular view of the structures by choosing View → Restore Camera View and restore a saved view by choosing View → Restore Camera View and then Previous, Next or a particular view.

Saved views are part of the project, and can be imported or exported. You can also make a movie out of a sequence of views. See the Manage Views Panel topic for more information.

To restore the default view of the Workspace, choose View → Reset All. This action resets the rotation, translation, and zoom of the Workspace to the default state, which has the screen axes along the coordinate axes of the Workspace structures.
