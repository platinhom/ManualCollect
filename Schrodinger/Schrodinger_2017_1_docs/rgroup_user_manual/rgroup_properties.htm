Analyzing Molecular Properties from R-Group Analysis

The R-group analysis facility offers several ways of displaying information on molecular properties and their relationship to the R-groups and the cores: an activity cliff plot, importance analysis, a heat map, R-group QSAR based on either pharmacophore features or electrotopological states (Estates), and an SAR table, in separate panels.
