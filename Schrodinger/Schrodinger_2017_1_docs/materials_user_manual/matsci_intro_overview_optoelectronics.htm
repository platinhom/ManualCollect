Optoelectronics Tools in the Materials Science Suite

The tools below calculate various optoelectronics-related properties.

  • Optoelectronic properties—calculate oxidation and reduction potentials, reorganization energies, triplet energies, and absorption spectra (Calculating Optoelectronic Properties)

  • Charge transfer rate—calculate the Marcus charge transfer rate for electrons or holes in an amorphous structure (Charge Transfer Rates)

  • Genetic optimization—optimize optoelectronic properties by mutating structures using a genetic algorithm (Optoelectronics Genetic Optimization Panel)

  • Spin states—calculate the lowest state of each spin for specified spin values, with empirical corrections for 3d transition metal complexes (Spin States Panel)

  • Amorphous properties—NMR shifts, singlet and triplet excitation energies and TADF with TDDFT in an amorphous cell (Amorphous Properties Panel)

  • Carrier transport—calculate carrier hopping rates in an amorphous structure (Electron Coupling Panel)

In addition, there are some service tools that may be useful:

  • Extract dimers—extract pairs of adjacent molecules from an amorphous structure, which can be used in calculations on the subsystem (Extract Dimers Panel)
