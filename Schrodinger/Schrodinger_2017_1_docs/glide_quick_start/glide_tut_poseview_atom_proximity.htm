Displaying Atoms by Proximity to the 1fjs Receptor

In this section, you will select a display that includes the ligand and the receptor residues nearest the ligand. This is useful for examining contacts and hydrogen bonds between the ligand and the active site of the receptor.

  1. Under Quick Select, click the L button (Select ligands (only))

  2. Right-click on the ligand and choose Expand Selection → Within 5 Å radius.

    The hidden receptor atoms within 5 Å of the ligand are selected. If you want more flexibility in picking the residues, you can choose Within Custom Radius and set the radius.

  3. In the Style toolbox, click the Display selected atoms only button.

    The ligand and the residues that are closest to the ligand are displayed. All residues that do not have any atoms within 5 Å of the ligand are undisplayed. Hiding the residues that do not come into contact with the ligand makes it easier to examine the ligand-receptor interactions.
