Setting Up the Display

Given the number of atoms in the typical receptor-ligand complex, it can be hard to identify the ligand. In this exercise you will locate the ligand and set up the display to view only the ligand and the protein residues close to it.

  1. Click the Select ligands (only) button under Quick Select on the toolbar.

    The ligand is selected.

  2. In the Styletoolbox, click the Tube button.

    The ligand is now displayed in the tube representation, and is clearly distinguished from the protein residues.

  3. Click Expand and choose Within 4 Å radius.

    The ligand plus residues within the chosen distance of the ligand are now selected.

  4. Click the Display selected atoms only button in the Style toolbox.

    Only the ligand and nearby residues are now displayed.

  5. Click the Fit to selection button.

    The view zooms in so that the structure fills most of the Workspace.

  6. Choose Element from the Color Atomsmenu in the Style toolbox.
