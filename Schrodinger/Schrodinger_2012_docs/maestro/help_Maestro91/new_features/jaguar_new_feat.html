New Features in Jaguar 7.9

    New Features

  • Jaguar is now available on Mac computers.

  • The D3 dispersion correction of Grimme et al. has been added for 16 density functionals, in addition to the B97-D functional of Grimme et al.

  • The total internal energy, enthalpy, and free energy are reported when you perform a frequency calculation.

  • When noncovalent interaction surfaces are requested, the bond critical points are identified and added to the output structure as dummy atoms, joined to the two bonded atoms by zero-order bonds.

  • Analysis of the average local ionization potential (ALIE) on the isodensity surface is performed to produce entry and atom properties (max, min, variance, etc.), similar to the ESP analysis.

  • Improved method for solvated gradients (isolvg=2) is now the default.

  • The pdftname keyword has been added for post-SCF DFT energy evaluations. It takes the same values as dftname.

  • Several additions have been made to the Spectrum Plot panel, including the ability to read an experimental spectrum and export spectra.

  • An analysis of the geometry optimization is added to the output by default. It is controlled by the keyword optverdict.

    • Other Changes

  • The idft and jdft keywords are no longer supported, though they are still available in the current release. You should change to using dftname and pdftname.
