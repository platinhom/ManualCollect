Tools Menu

The Tools menu contains tools for creating atom sets, performing measurements on structures, superimposing structures, filtering and aligning ligands, analyzing R-groups on ligands, aligning protein structures, assigning secondary structures of proteins, bond orders and partial charges, scanning torsions and rotamer states, minimizing structures, and various viewing and plotting tools. Most of these items open panels. The exceptions are the Assign items, Minimize, and Structure Sculpting. The menu items are listed below, with links to the relevant topics.

  • View in PyMOL—Open the PyMOL for Maestro panel, which allows you to start PyMOL, load structures from Maestro to PyMOL, and make settings for the PyMOL scene and image.
  • Sets
  • Ligand Filtering
  • R-Group Analysis
  • Measurements—Submenu with items to pick measurements, open the Measurements panel for various kinds of measurements, reposition measurement markers, and delete measurements.
  • H-Bonds and Contacts
  • Protein Reports
  • Ramachandran Plot
  • Ligand Interaction Diagram
  • Superposition
  • Flexible Ligand Alignment
  • Protein Structure Alignment
  • Align Binding Sites
  • Assign Secondary Structure
  • Assign Bond Orders
  • Assign Partial Charges
  • Minimize
  • Rapid Torsion Scan
  • Structure Sculpting
  • Plot Coordinate Scan → One Coordinate, Two Coordinates
  • Force Field Viewer
  • Multiple Sequence Viewer
