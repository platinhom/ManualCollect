Summary

The "Electrostatic" panel is used to display all the electrostatic interactions for a given calculation.

Opening the Electrostatic Panel

To open the Electrostatic panel, click the Electrostatic button in the Force Field Viewer Panel.

Using the Electrostatic Panel

The Electrostatic Interactions List, located on the left side of the "Electrostatic" panel, displays all the electrostatic interactions present in the currently selected .mmo file in the order they were read from the file. To select a particular interaction, highlight it in the list by clicking on it with the left mouse button, or pick the atoms defining it from the Workspace structure. The text boxes on the panel will update to display data relevant to the selected interaction.

Electrostatic Panel Features

  • Electrostatic Interactions List
  • Find by Picking
  • Sort by Energy
  • Show Force Field
  • Electrostatic Energy
  • Distance
  • Charge
Electrostatic Interactions List:

All the electrostatic interactions in a given .mmo file are displayed in the Electrostatic Interactions List located on the left side of the "Electrostatic" panel. The text box in which the list appears is a read-only field.

Find by Picking:

To find a particular interaction in the list, enable the "Find by Picking" toggle button and click on the two atoms in the structure that define the interaction. Once the second atom has been picked, the interaction corresponding to those two atoms will be selected in the list.

Sort by Energy:

By default, the electrostatic interactions that are displayed in the list appear in the order they were read from the .mmo file. However, the list can be sorted according to energy by enabling the "Sort By Energy" toggle button. The interaction with the lowest (i.e. most negative) energy will then be displayed at the beginning of the list and the interaction with the highest energy (i.e. most positive) will be displayed at the end.

Show Force Field:

Clicking on the "Show Force Field" button opens the "FField" panel, which displays the selected .mmo file in its entirety. The line selected in the force field file display corresponds to the electrostatic interaction that is currently selected in the Electrostatic Interactions List.

Electrostatic Energy:

The numeric value displayed in this text field represents the energy penalty for the electrostatic interaction of two atoms. The energy value is usually calculated from the partial atomic charges and interatomic distance of the concerned atom pair. Electrostatic interactions are only calculated for atoms that are at least two bonds apart, but that are closer to each other than the specified cutoff distance.

Distance:

"Distance:" refers to how far (in angstroms) the members of the currently selected atom pair are from each other.

Charge:

The "Charge:" value is the product of the partial charges of atoms in the currently selected pair.
