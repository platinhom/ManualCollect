Adding Entries to the Workspace

To include the selected entries in the Workspace in addition to the entries already displayed, choose Entry → Include.

Maestro maintains an association between the included entries and their respective Workspace atoms, in order to track changes made in the Workspace. By default, included entries are displayed in the Workspace, and changes made to them in the Workspace are saved back to the project. However, you may elect to undisplay some entry atoms or choose not to have changes saved back into the project.

The box in the In column of the Project Table panel is filled in for included entries.
