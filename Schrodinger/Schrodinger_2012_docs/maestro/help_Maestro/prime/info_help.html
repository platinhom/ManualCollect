Prime General Help

Prime Online Help includes:

  • a topic for each Prime-Structure Prediction (Prime-SP) step, which can be viewed by clicking the Help button in the panel for the step;
  • topics providing general information about using Prime-SP and about Prime generally (see Related Topics below);
  • topics for the standalone refinement module, Prime-Refinement, and Prime MM-GBSA module.

Online help topics for Prime can also be opened from the main menu bar:

  1. Choose Help → Online Help to open the main help topic in your browser.
  2. In the navigation pane (on the left), click Prime. A list of Prime topics is displayed in this pane. When you click a topic in this list, it is displayed in the content pane (on the right).
