Saving and Restoring the View

When you rotate, translate, or zoom in on the structures in the Workspace, the actual coordinates of the structures are not changed, only the view of the structures: the "camera angle". Maestro stores the current view as a transformation that is applied to the coordinates. You can save a particular view of the structures by clicking the Save view button on the Saved Views toolbar, and restore a saved view by clicking one of the other toolbar buttons (Previous, Next or a view number).

Saved views are part of the project, and can be imported or exported. You can also make a movie out of a sequence of views. See the Manage Views Panel topic for more information.
