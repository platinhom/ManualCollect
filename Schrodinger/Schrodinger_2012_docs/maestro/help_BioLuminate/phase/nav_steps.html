Navigating Steps in Phase

Phase provides several ways of navigating the steps in the Develop Common Pharmacophore Hypotheses panel. The normal workflow takes you sequentially through the steps, but you can go to any step for which you have the necessary data, and continue working from that point. This enables you to leave a run at a particular point and return to it later. The three ways of navigating the steps are given below.

  • The Back and Next buttons

    To go to the next step, click the Next button; to go to the previous step, click the Back button.

  • The Guide

    The Guide displays the steps in the model as a set of buttons linked by lines. The buttons for the steps that are not available are dimmed. The current step is highlighted with a white background. You can go to any available step by clicking its button in the Guide. The Guide can be displayed or hidden from the Step menu.

  • The Step menu

    The steps are listed on the Step menu. The items for the steps that are not available are dimmed. You can go to any available step by choosing the corresponding menu item.
