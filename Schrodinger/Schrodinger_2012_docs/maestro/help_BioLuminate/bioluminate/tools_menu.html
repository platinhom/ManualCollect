Tools Menu

  • Protein Preparation—Prepare proteins for use in modeling, with the Protein Preparation Wizard.

  • Protein Structure Quality—Present graphical and numeric analyses of protein quality, including reports on protein properties, such as geometric deviations from ideal values, G-factors, B-factors, stereochemistry, planarity, missing atoms, and a Ramachandran plot.

  • Protein Consensus Viewer—Display sequence consensus information. Opens the Consensus Visualization panel.

  • Reactive Residue Identification—Identify protein residues that are likely to be reactive. Opens the Reactive Protein Residues panel.

  • Ligand Interaction Diagram—View a 2D diagram of the interactions between the ligand and the receptor that are in the Workspace, in the Ligand Interaction Diagram panel.

  • Superposition—Superimpose structures, using the Superposition panel.

  • Binding Site Alignment—Align the binding sites of two or more proteins, in the Align Binding Sites panel.

  • Protein Structure Alignment—Align two or more protein structures, based on sequence and secondary structure in the Protein Structure Alignment panel.

  • Create and Manage Surfaces—Generate, import, and display surfaces. Submenu for selection of the surface type, import surfaces, hide surfaces, and manage surfaces and their appearance. See the Surfaces category for information on the submenu items.

  • Binding Site Identification—Identify possible ligand binding sites on a protein, with SiteMap.

  • Multiple Sequence Viewer—View and perform tasks on multiple protein sequences, such as alignment, analysis, and homology modeling, in the Multiple Sequence Viewer panel.
