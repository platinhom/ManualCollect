Style Menu

The Style menu controls the appearance of the atoms and bonds in the Workspace, the creation of predefined styles (atom and bond representation, coloring, ribbons, atom visbility and so on), the display of extra properties of the Workspace structure such as surfaces or measurements of distances and angles, and annotation of the Workspace structure.

    • Apply Predefined StyleApply the current predefined style to the contents of the Workspace. Also available from the Style toolbar.

    • Predefined Style Settings—Set up a predefined style. Submenu has items for setting up the style, defining the ligand, choosing a rendering priority (speed vs quality), setting the kind of material atoms and bonds appear to be made of (via lighting effects), and fixing the receptor in the Workspace. Also available from the Style toolbar.

    • Auto Apply Predefined Style—Automatically reapply the current predefined style when the contents of the Workspace change. Also available from the Style toolbar.

    • Atom and Bond Representation—Change the representation of atoms, bonds, and ribbons, in the Molecular Representation panel.

    • Ribbons—Change ribbon representations. Opens the Ribbons panel.

    • Create and Manage Surfaces—Generate, import, and display surfaces. Submenu for selection of the surface type, import surfaces, hide surfaces, and manage surfaces and their appearance. See the Surfaces category for information on the submenu items.

    • Atom and Bond Coloring—Change the coloring of atoms and bonds, in the Atom and Bond Coloring panel.

    • Atom Labels—Set up and display atom labels, in the Atom Labels panel.

    • Measurements—Measure distances, angles, dihedrals; display H-bonds, contacts and spin-spin couplings. Submenu with items to pick atoms for measurements, open the Measurements panel for various kinds of measurements, reposition measurement markers, and delete measurements.

    • Add Caption—Add a caption to the Workspace. Opens the New Caption dialog box, in which you can type in the text, and set the caption background, font, and text color.

    • Delete Annotation—Remove different kinds of annotation from the Workspace. Submenu lists the kinds of annotation you can remove.

    • Highlights, Text and Arrows—Create, modify, delete, or apply grapnical highlights to atoms in the Workspace, with or without text and arrows. Opens the Manage Highlights panel.
