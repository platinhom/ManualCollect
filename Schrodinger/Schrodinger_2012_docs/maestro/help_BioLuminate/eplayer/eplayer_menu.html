Project Table — ePlayer Menu

This menu provides access controls for the ePlayer (which are duplicated in the toolbar) and allows you to set options for play and actions to take during play.

Go to Start   Display the first selected entry (lowest row number) in the entry selection.
Previous   Display the previous entry in the list of selected entries.
Play Backward   Display the selected entries in sequence, moving toward the first.
Stop   Stop the ePlayer.
Play Forward   Display the selected entries in sequence, moving toward the last.
Next   Display the next entry in the list of selected entries.
Go to End   Display the last selected entry (highest row number) in the entry selection.
Loop   Repeat the display of the entries. The submenu offers three choices:
  • None—Stop after playing through the entries: do not repeat the play sequence.
  • Single Direction—When play reaches the last entry in the direction of play, go to the other end and play through them again, and repeat this process until play is stopped.
  • Oscillate—When play reaches the last entry in the direction of play, reverse the direction of play and play through the entries to the other end of the list. Repeat this process until play is stopped.
Previous Group   Display the first entry of the previous group in the entry selection.
Next Group   Display the first entry of the next group in the entry selection.
Options   Open the ePlayer Options panel in which you can set options for speed, saving and superposition of entries, and scripts to run for each entry.
