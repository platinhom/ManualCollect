Summary

The SA Solvation panel is used to display, for each atom, the Surface Area (SA) component of the GB/SA calculation.

Opening the Force Field Viewer's SA Solvation Panel

To open the Force Field Viewer's SA Solvation panel, you can:

  • Click SA Solvation in the Force Field Viewer panel.

Using the Force Field Viewer's SA Solvation Panel

The Solvated Atom List, located on the left side of the SA Solvation panel displays all of the solvated atoms present in the selected .mmo file in the order they were read from the file. To select a particular atom, highlight it in the list by clicking on it with the left mouse button, or pick it from the Workspace structure. The text boxes on the panel will update to display data relevant to the selected atom.

Solvated Atom List:

All the solvated atoms in a given .mmo file are displayed in the Solvated Atom List located on the left side of the "SA Solvation" panel. The text box in which the list appears is a read-only field.

Find by Picking:

To find a particular atom in the list, enable the "Find by Picking" toggle button and click on the solvated atom on the structure in the Workspace. Once the atom has been picked (assuming an entry for it can be found), the corresponding atom will be selected in the Solvated Atom List.

Sort by Energy:

By default, the atoms that are displayed in the Solvated Atoms List appear in the order they were read from the .mmo file. However, the list may be sorted according to energy by enabling the "Sort By Energy" toggle button. The atom with the lowest (i.e. most negative) energy will then be displayed at the beginning of the list and the atom with the highest energy (i.e. most positive) will be displayed at the end.

Show Force Field:

Clicking on the "Show Force Field" button opens the "FField" panel, which displays the currently selected .mmo file in its entirety. The selected line in the force field file display corresponds to the atom that is currently selected in the Solvated Atom List.

Energy:

The numeric value displayed in this text field represents the solvation energy of the currently selected atom as calculated by the Surface Area portion of the GB/SA model.

Area:

The number in the "Area:" text box is value used to calculate the solvent-accessible surface area of the atom.

Radius:

The "Radius:" box displays the radius that was used to calculate the solvent-accessible surface area of the atom.

Atom Parameter:

This field is not currently supported by the MacroModel program. The value that appears here is 0.000.

Energy Multiplier:

The Surface Area energy for an atom is simply the product of the solvent-accessible surface area of the atom and a proportionality constant that is based on atom type. The number in the "Energy Multiplier:" field is the value of the proportionality constant for that atom.
