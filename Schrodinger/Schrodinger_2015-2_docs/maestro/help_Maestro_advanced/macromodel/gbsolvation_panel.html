Summary

The GB Solvation panel is used to display, for each atom, the Generalized Born (GB) component of the GB/SA calculation.

Opening the Force Field Viewer's GB Solvation Panel

To open the Force Field Viewer's GB Solvation panel, you can:

  • Click GB Solvation in the Force Field Viewer panel.

Using the Force Field Viewer's GB Solvation Panel

The Solvated Atom List on the left side of the GB Solvation panel displays all the solvated atoms present the selected .mmo file in the order they were read from the file. To select a particular atom, highlight it in the list by clicking on it with the left mouse button, or pick it from the Workspace structure. The text boxes on the panel will update to display data relevant to the selected atom.

Solvated Atom List:

All the solvated atoms in a given .mmo file are displayed in the Solvated Atom List located on the left side of the "GB Solvation" panel. The text box in which the list appears is a read-only field.

Find by Picking:

To find a particular atom in the list, enable the "Find by Picking" toggle button and click on the solvated atom on the structure. Once the atom has been picked (and assuming an entry for it can be found), the corresponding atom will be selected in the Solvated Atom List.

Sort by Energy:

By default, the atoms whose numbers are displayed in the Solvated Atoms List appear in the order they were read from the .mmo file. However, the list can be sorted according to energy by enabling the "Sort By Energy" toggle button. The atom with the lowest (i.e. most negative) energy will then be displayed at the beginning of the list and the atom with the highest energy (i.e. most positive) will be displayed at the end.

Show Force Field:

Clicking on the "Show Force Field" button opens the "FField" panel, which displays the .mmo file in its entirety. The line that is selected in the force field file display corresponds to the atom that is currently selected in the Solvated Atom List.

Energy:

The numeric value displayed in this text field represents the solvation energy of the currently selected atom as calculated by the Generalized Born portion of the GB/SA model. The GB term in the GB/SA treatment accounts for the solvate-solvent electrostatic interactions.

Volume:

The number in the "Volume:" field can generally be thought of as the volume of a sphere calculated using the Born Radius of the selected atom.

Radius:

The value that appears in the "Radius:" text box is the radius of the atom. Note that this value may be different than that used to calculate the solvent-accessible surface area, since the latter may be increased to include attached hydrogens.

Born Radius:

The Born radius of an atom is essentially an effective radius that is based on the distance of an atom from the solvent front. An atom's contribution to the molecular solvation energy is inversely proportional to this radius. That is, atoms that are buried, and therefore screened from the solvent, make smaller contributions to the GB solvation energy.
