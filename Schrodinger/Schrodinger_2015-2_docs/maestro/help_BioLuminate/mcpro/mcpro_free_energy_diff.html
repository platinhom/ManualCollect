MCPRO+ Free-Energy Difference Calculations

In MCPRO+ free-energy difference calculations, the free-energy difference between two structures is obtained by free-energy perturbation. The setup and requirements are laid out in the topic MCPRO+ Free-Energy Perturbation Calculations.

You can set up and run a free-energy difference calculation in the MCPRO+ Free-Energy Difference by FEP panel, which you open by choosing Applications → MCPRO+ → Free Energy Difference by FEP. The general layout and common features of this panel are described in the topic MCPRO+ Panels.
