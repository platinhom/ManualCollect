Spot Centering

Spot-centering changes the view so that the selected atom is at the center of the Workspace, and makes the chosen atom the global center of rotation. It has no effect on any local center of rotation. Spot-centering does not affect the molecular coordinates.

To spot-center an atom, right-click on the atom.
