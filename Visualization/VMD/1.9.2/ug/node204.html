VMD Script Commands for Colors

In order to fine tune color parameters, one typically needs more sophisticated controls than those offered in the GUI. For this reason, VMD provides a number of scripting level commands for color access. These commands will be discussed in detail in chapter 9, but to give you a flavor for their use, here are a couple of examples that you may find useful right away.
