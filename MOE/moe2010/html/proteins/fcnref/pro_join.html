MOE 2010.10

Protein Functions

Syntax

    pro_Join chain_keys

Description

Concatenates the chains specified in chain_keys. The second chain is appended to the first, the third to the result, and so forth; i. e., the chains are concatenated from left to right. The chains are reparented to the parent of the first chain. Peptide bonds between joined chains are created if they do not already exist. 

    svl> pdb_Open [ '101M', '117E.A' ]
    svl> pro_Join Chains []
