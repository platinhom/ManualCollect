E-mail:

Click the "Try it" button to allow multiple values (emails) for the email field on form submission.

Tip: Try to type in more than one email before and after you have clicked on the "Try it" button. Separate each email with a comma, like: mail@example.com, mail2@example.com.

Note: input elements with type="email" are not supported in IE 9 (and earlier), or Safari.
